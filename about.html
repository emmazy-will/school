About DeHills School

Our history, mission, and values

Our History

Founded in 1985, DeHills School has been a pillar of academic excellence in our community for over three decades. What began as a small neighborhood school has grown into a premier educational institution serving students from preschool through high school.

Our founder, Dr. Eleanor DeHills, envisioned a school that would combine rigorous academics with character development and creative expression. Today, we continue to uphold her vision while adapting to the needs of 21st-century learners.

Our Mission

At DeHills School, our mission is to nurture curious, compassionate, and confident learners who are prepared to meet the challenges of an ever-changing world. We believe in educating the whole child - mind, body, and character.

Our Values

  • Excellence in academics and character
  • Respect for self and others
  • Commitment to community service
  • Celebration of diversity
  • Lifelong love of learning
