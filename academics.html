Academic Programs

Excellence in education at every level

Our Curriculum

At DeHills School, we offer a comprehensive, challenging curriculum designed to prepare students for success in college and beyond. Our program emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and real-world application of knowledge.

Elementary School (Grades K-5)

Our elementary program builds a strong foundation in core academic areas while nurturing curiosity and a love of learning. Key features include:

  • Literacy-focused curriculum with small reading groups
  • Hands-on mathematics using Singapore Math approach
  • Integrated science and social studies units
  • Specialist classes in art, music, physical education, and Spanish
  • Social-emotional learning program

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Our middle school program helps students transition to more advanced coursework while providing the support they need during these formative years. Highlights include:

  • Departmentalized instruction with subject-area experts
  • Advanced math tracks available
  • STEM lab and research projects
  • Elective options including coding, drama, and journalism
  • Advisory program for academic and social support

High School (Grades 9-12)

Our college-preparatory high school program offers rigorous academics with opportunities for specialization. Features include:

  • Honors and Advanced Placement courses
  • Dual enrollment with local universities
  • Comprehensive STEM and humanities programs
  • Senior capstone project requirement
  • College counseling beginning in freshman year
