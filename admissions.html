Welcome Future DeHills Families

We're delighted you're considering DeHills School for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to help us get to know your family and ensure DeHills is the right fit for your child's needs and aspirations.

Admissions Process

1

Inquiry

Submit an inquiry form or attend an open house to learn more about our programs.

Inquire Now
2

Application

Complete our online application and submit required documents.

Start Application
3

Assessment

Students participate in age-appropriate assessments or classroom visits.

4

Interview

Family interview with admissions staff (for middle and high school applicants).

5

Decision

Admissions decisions are communicated according to our timeline.

Requirements by Grade Level

Elementary School

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Teacher recommendation (Grades 1-5)
  • Readiness assessment (Kindergarten)

Middle School

  • Completed application form
  • Transcript from current school
  • Two teacher recommendations
  • Admissions assessment
  • Family interview

High School

  • Completed application form
  • Transcript from current school
  • Three teacher recommendations
  • Admissions test (SSAT or ISEE)
  • Student and family interviews
  • Writing sample

Tuition & Fees

DeHills School is committed to making our exceptional education accessible to families from diverse economic backgrounds. Tuition for the 2023-2024 academic year:

Grade Level Annual Tuition
Kindergarten $18,500
Grades 1-5 $22,000
Grades 6-8 $25,000
Grades 9-12 $28,000

* Additional fees may apply for certain programs and activities.

Financial Aid

Approximately 30% of DeHills students receive some form of financial assistance. We offer need-based financial aid to qualified families.

  • Financial aid applications are due February 1st
  • Awards are granted based on demonstrated need
  • Sibling discounts available
  • Payment plans offered
