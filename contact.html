Location & Directions

From Downtown

Take Highway 101 North to Exit 45. Turn right onto Education Avenue. The school will be on your left after 1 mile.

Public Transportation

The #45 bus stops directly in front of the school. The nearest subway station is Learning City Station (Blue Line), a 10-minute walk.

Parking

Visitor parking is available in the front lot. Additional parking is available in the rear lot during school hours.
